Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 28, 2026  ·  Last updated: March 28, 2026

The short version: ParkPin saves your parked car's location on your device only. We do not collect, store, or sell your personal data. Location coordinates are used solely to display your pin on the map and calculate a walking route back to your car.

1. Who We Are

ParkPin is a mobile application that helps you remember where you parked your car. It is developed and operated as an independent app. If you have questions about this policy, contact us at privacy@parkpin.app.

2. Information We Collect

Location Data

ParkPin requests access to your device's GPS location for two purposes:

  • Saving your parked location — when you tap "Pin My Car", your current GPS coordinates are saved locally on your device using on-device SQLite storage. No location data is sent to our servers.
  • Calculating a walking route — when you request directions back to your car, your current location and your pinned location are sent to a secure server-side proxy to retrieve a walking route from Google Maps. These coordinates are used only to compute the route and are not stored or logged.
Crash Reports

ParkPin uses Firebase Crashlytics (by Google) to collect anonymized crash reports when the app experiences an unexpected error. This data includes device model, OS version, and a stack trace of the crash. It does not include your name, email, or location.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not require account registration or collect your name, email, or any personal identifiers.
  • We do not store your location history on any server.
  • We do not track your movements or build a profile of where you have been.
  • We do not serve ads and do not share data with advertisers.
  • We do not sell your data to any third party.

4. How Your Data Is Stored

Your pinned location is stored in an on-device SQLite database. This data never leaves your device except when you explicitly request walking directions (see Section 2). Clearing the pin or uninstalling the app permanently removes all stored data.

5. Third-Party Services

ParkPin uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy practices:

  • Google Maps Platform — used to display maps and calculate walking routes. When you request directions, your origin and destination coordinates are processed by Google. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Crashlytics (Google) — anonymized crash reporting. See Firebase Privacy Policy.

6. Children's Privacy

ParkPin is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

Since ParkPin stores all data locally on your device, you have full control over your data at all times. You can:

  • Clear your pinned location at any time within the app.
  • Revoke location permission at any time in your device settings.
  • Delete all app data by uninstalling ParkPin.

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under GDPR including the right to access, rectify, or erase your data. Contact us at hbilalcam@gmail.com to exercise these rights.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
